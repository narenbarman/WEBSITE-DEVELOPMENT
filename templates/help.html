Help & Support Center

Welcome to Barman Store's Help & Support Center. Here you'll find answers to common questions and information about our services.

Getting Started

If you're new to Barman Store, here's how to get started:

  • Create an Account: Click on "Account" in the navigation and register with your details.
  • Browse Products: Visit our "Catalog" to see all available products.
  • Add to Cart: Click "Add to Cart" on any product you want to purchase.
  • Checkout: Review your cart and proceed to checkout when ready.

Shopping Guide

How to Shop

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Browse our product catalog
  3. Add items to your cart
  4. Review your cart and proceed to checkout
  5. Your order will be processed and you'll receive confirmation

Payment Information

We currently support cash on delivery for all orders. Payment is collected when your order is delivered.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on "Account" in the navigation bar, then click "Register here" to create a new account. You'll need to provide your name, email, phone number, address, and password.
How do I add items to my cart?
Browse the catalog, find the product you want, specify the quantity, and click "Add to Cart". You can view your cart by clicking the cart icon in the navigation.
How do I place an order?
After adding items to your cart, click on the cart link in the navigation. Review your items, then click "Checkout" to place your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
Currently, we accept cash on delivery. Payment is collected when your order is delivered to your address.
How can I track my order?
You can view your order status by logging into your account and checking your order history. Admins can also view all orders in the admin panel.
How do I contact customer support?
For any questions or issues, please contact our support team using the information provided below.

Admin Features

If you have administrator privileges, you have access to additional features:

  • Manage Products: Add, edit, and delete products from the catalog
  • Manage Users: View and edit user accounts
  • View Orders: See all customer orders and update their status
  • Purchase Register: Track supplier purchases and inventory
  • Bill Management: Generate and print bills for orders

To access admin features, log in with an admin account and look for the "Admin Panel" link.
